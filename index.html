What are we even doing?


We're making biscuit here. In Swiss German these are called "Mailänderli". I will prepare these cookies with you step by step. So no mistakes can happen. Keep in mind that you need these Tools:

  • Cookie cutter
  • Rolling wood
  • Bowls
  • Cling film

If you have all these you are good to go:)


Preparation


Everything you need: 125g butter, 115g sugar, 10g vanilla sugar, lemon peel, an egg and 250g flour.

Step 1.


First you have to put the butter, sugar, vanilla sugar, lemon peel and egg together in a bowl. It should look like in the picture.

Step 2.


When you have everything in the bowl you can mix it up with a whisk. When you are finished it should look like the picture. If it doesn't look like it, you should keep stirring.

Step 3.


After you have finished mixing, you can add the 250g flour. It looks like too much flour, but it will spread out later. I would also be careful that no flour flies out of the bowl.
