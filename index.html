What are we even doing?


We're making biscuit here. In Swiss German these are called "Mailänderli". I will prepare these cookies with you step by step. So no mistakes can happen. Keep in mind that you need these Tools:

  • Cookie cutter
  • Rolling wood
  • Bowls
  • Cling film

If you have all these you are good to go:)


Preparation


Everything you need: 125g butter, 115g sugar, 10g vanilla sugar, lemon peel, an egg and 250g flour.

Step 1.


First you have to put the butter, sugar, vanilla sugar, lemon peel and egg together in a bowl. It should look like in the picture.

Step 2.


When you have everything in the bowl you can mix it up with a whisk. When you are finished it should look like the picture. If it doesn't look like it, you should keep stirring.

Step 3.


After you have finished mixing, you can add the 250g flour. It looks like too much flour, but it will spread out later. I would also be careful that no flour flies out of the bowl.

Step 4.


Now you can mix the flour and the rest with your hands. You shouldn't mix the whole thing too long, otherwise it will be more difficult when rolling out the dough.

Step 5.


When you're done mixing, you can wrap the dough in cling film and put it in the refrigerator for at least two hours. After these two hours, your dough will be ready.

Step 6.


After these at least two hours, you can put some flour on the table and put the dough on it. The flour is there to prevent the dough from sticking.

Step 7.


Then you can roll out the dough to about 7mm. Don't forget to put a little flour on the rolling wood so that the dough doesn't stick to it.

Step 8.


After you have rolled out the dough, you can use the cutters to cut out figures from the dough. If you run out of space you can repeat the steps above until you run out of dough.

Step 9.


After you have used up the dough, you can put all the figures on a tray. Don't forget to put tin paper underneath, otherwise the biscuits will stick to the tin.

Step 10.


When you have all the cookies on the tray you can paint them with egg yolk and put them in the oven. You have to leave the biscuits inside at 175 ° C for about 10 minutes.

Done!!!!


Now you can let the biscuits cool down a little. Then you're done and can eat them. Congratulations you have finished your "Mailänderli".
